# WellnessWave 

## Project Title
WellnessWave – Online Medical & Wellness Store

---

## Project Idea

WellnessWave is an innovative online medical and wellness store designed to bring a complete range of healthcare products right to users' doorsteps. The platform aims to replicate the experience of a traditional medical store in a convenient and accessible digital format. 

Users can browse, search, and purchase a wide range of medicines, wellness products, and healthcare essentials from the comfort of their homes. The platform emphasizes convenience, security, and a user-friendly interface, ensuring healthcare accessibility for everyone.

---

## Brief Description

WellnessWave is built to provide a safe, seamless, and reliable online pharmacy experience. The platform enables users to explore various healthcare products with detailed descriptions and images, search efficiently, and complete purchases through a secure checkout system.

This project will integrate frontend design, backend logic, and database management to create a full-stack e-commerce healthcare application. The goal is to ensure a smooth user experience while maintaining high security standards for transactions and user data.

---

## Core Features

### 1️⃣ Wide Product Selection
WellnessWave offers a broad range of healthcare products including medicines, vitamins, supplements, and wellness essentials. Users can browse through categories and view detailed descriptions along with product images.

### 2️⃣ Easy Search & Filtering
Users can search for products by name or category and apply filters based on price and availability. This ensures that finding the right product is quick and simple.

### 3️⃣ Secure Checkout & Multiple Payment Options
The platform provides a secure checkout process with multiple payment methods. All user and payment information is encrypted to ensure safe and protected transactions.
